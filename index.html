CircO2 works by restoring a compound the body produces less of with age: nitric oxide.   1. Lozenge dissolves in the mouth: As the lozenge slowly dissolves, active nitrate-containing compounds come into contact with saliva and the beneficial bacteria on the tongue and oral mucosa, initiating the first stage of conversion.
